Imagine if every light bulb in your home was using ten times more energy than needed, just because it could.
That would waste said energy, cost money and damage the environment.

    What happens:
  • Websites that are bloated use more server resources, more energy, more data (what about people on limited plans?), which is both costly and environmentally damaging.
    • What you do:
  • Optimize those light bulbs (your website code) so they only use as much energy as needed, making Everything more efficient.
  • Stop being lazy.

Result:
Lower costs, faster load times and a more sustainable web.

Compare for yourself between the good and bad examples being collected.
